About this tool

Enter any English word to see its part of speech along with a definition and example sentence. After exploring a few words you can test yourself with the quiz.

FAQs

Where do the definitions come from?

Definitions and sample sentences are fetched from dictionaryapi.dev whenever possible.

What happens if a word isn’t found?

If the dictionary lacks an entry, the tool will note that no results were found and you won’t receive quiz points.
