About this tool

Quickly estimate whether your B variant beats A. Enter the visitors and conversions from your experiment and the calculator runs a two-tailed z-test.

FAQs

What counts as significant?

We flag results as significant when the p-value is below 0.05 (95% confidence).

Should I stop my test?

Only stop a test early if you have strong significance and enough traffic to be confident in the result.
